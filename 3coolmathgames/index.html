3 Awesome Coolmath Games!

Curated By Hisham Ettayebi

Coolmath games is a website that is home to over 2,000 games that are ALL free to play for anyone to enjoy. Their games are known for involving challenges that require skills, whether they are puzzle-solving skills, quick thinking, or just speed and accuracy, Coolmath games always has something fun AND challenging for everyone to enjoy. This website will show you 3 games on Coolmath games that you may or may not have played before: World's Hardest Game, Run, and OvO.

You play as a red square that must move through levels that become increasingly difficult. The main obstacles are blue dots that move around through each course. If one of them touches the square, you respawn either at the beginning of the level or at the most recent checkpoint you passed in the level.

You play as an alien who can run on the floor, walls, and ceiling. You run through complicated courses in space that become increasingly difficult and have to avoid falling through the holes in the surfaces you are running on.

In this 2D adventure game, you play as a guy who navigates obstacle courses that become increasingly difficult by jumping from platform to platform, climbing walls, and jumping over spikes that kill upon impact.
