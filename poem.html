The Exciting Sport of Formula 1

Formula 1 is an exhilarating sport filled with high speeds, g-forces, and emotions, but in order to love the sport, you need to get to know it first.  The F1 season consists of 24 Grands Prix all around the world, starting in March and ending in December. With ten teams and two drivers per team, drivers and constructors race to win 2 championships, the drivers championship and the constructors championship. The drivers championship is won by the individual driver with the most points at the end of the season and the constructors championship is won by the team with the most points between both of their drivers combined.
